OUR STORY

Founded in 1985, Indian Catering Service began as a small family-owned business with a passion for authentic Indian cuisine. Our journey started in a modest kitchen, where our founder, Mrs. Sharma, would prepare delicious meals for local events and gatherings. Word of her exceptional cooking spread quickly, and soon, the demand for her catering services grew beyond our wildest dreams.

Over the years, we've expanded our team, refined our recipes, and embraced modern catering techniques while staying true to our traditional roots. Today, we're proud to be one of the leading Indian catering services in the region, serving a wide range of clients from intimate family gatherings to large corporate events.

Our commitment to quality, flavor, and customer satisfaction remains at the heart of everything we do. We continue to innovate and create new culinary experiences while honoring the rich tapestry of Indian cuisine. Join us on this flavorful journey and let us make your next event an unforgettable culinary adventure!
